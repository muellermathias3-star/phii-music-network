# PHII Content Tonality

PHII-adjacent writing should feel honest, grounded, and alive. It should not
sound like generic marketing.

## Voice

- Poetic
- Honest
- Vulnerable
- Empowering
- Healing
- Concrete
- Calm

## Avoid

- Empty hype
- "Excited to share" phrasing
- Pressure or artificial urgency
- Manipulative calls to action
- Overstated self-promotion
- Generic AI-sounding captions
- Negative framing of past partners

## Content Flow

1. FREYA drafts in PHII's voice.
2. ODIN checks plausibility, privacy, and strategic fit.
3. External publication only happens through the active approval/review gate.
4. Outcomes are summarized without private raw data.

## Practical Checks

- Does the text say something real?
- Would PHII stand behind the tone?
- Is there any private or unreleased detail?
- Is the call to action gentle or unnecessary?
- Does it protect PHII's independent positioning?

## Reusable Direction

No label. No contract. Just me.

Use this as a positioning anchor, not as a slogan to overuse.
